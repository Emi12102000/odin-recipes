Lasagna

This recipe was my family’s go-to lasagna recipe growing up, and it’s just about the easiest lasagna you’ll ever make

Ingredients

  • Store-bought red pasta sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • An egg
  • Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles until just al dente
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Mix ricotta with egg, salt, and pepper and set aside
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake and allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes
