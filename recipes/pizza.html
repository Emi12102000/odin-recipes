Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 150 grams Salami of your choice
  • ½ cup grated Gouda cheese
  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup homemade marinara sauce
  • 350 grams homemade pizza dough

Steps

  1. First prepare the dough according to the recipe
  2. Prepare the homemade marinara sauce
  3. Preheat the oven to 240°C (460°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  4. On a floured surface roll out the pizza dough to the thickness of your liking. I rolled mine to about 0.5 inch thick. The dough will rise a bit while baking as well
  5. Spread the marinara sauce all over the rolled out dough. Sprinkle the shredded Gouda cheese. Top with mozzarella cheese
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown
  7. Serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy
